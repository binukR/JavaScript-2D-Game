How to play


To play Mindful Journey, press the 'Enter' key to start the game. You can use the 'Space' key to jump during mini-games. Explore the different activities available and complete tasks to promote relaxation and mindfulness. Let the calming visuals, sounds, and gameplay transport you to a peaceful and stress-free state.
